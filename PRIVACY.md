# Privacy Policy for Spinyl

**Last updated:** December 28, 2025

**Spinyl - Vinyl Music Player** ("the App") is developed by Viki Kaasinen. This privacy policy describes how your personal data is handled.

## 1. Data Collection
The App **does not** collect, store, or share any personal information, location data, or usage statistics to external servers owned by the developer. All data processing related to music playback happens locally on your device or directly through Spotify's services.

## 2. Spotify Integration
The App uses the **Spotify iOS SDK** to play music.
* To use the App, you must authorize it with your Spotify account.
* This authentication process is handled directly by Spotify. The App receives an access token to control playback, but it never sees or stores your Spotify password.
* Please refer to [Spotify's Privacy Policy](https://www.spotify.com/legal/privacy-policy/) for information on how they handle your data.

## 3. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please [contact](https://support.uomagrit.com/spinyl) the developer.
